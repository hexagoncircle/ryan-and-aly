Ryan & Aly (Or, the Mulligabes)

In June 2021, we got married in our neighborhood bar.

Jersey City is a really important place to us. While we met in Seattle, we fell in love in NJ over a dozen different trips Ryan made to see friends and family. Frank, a long time close friend of Ryan, conveniently lived a few blocks from Aly – all the more reason to be in the neighborhood!

It’s the city where we had our first date, where we got engaged, where we sheltered-in-place when the pandemic hit, and now, the city on our marriage license. We made it official at the bar around the corner from Aly’s apartment, surrounded by a few of our closest friends.

A big thank you to Ashley, Brooke, Vicky, Frank, and April for sharing these moments with us.

One month later, we exchanged vows in a tiny desert ceremony.

For us, one of the only important aspects of having a wedding ceremony was the chance for our two families to come together. We feel so fortunate to each have such loving parents and wonderful siblings. So everyone from Seattle, North Carolina, Las Vegas, and Los Angeles made their way out to Joshua Tree for a good old-fashioned pizza puppy pool party.
